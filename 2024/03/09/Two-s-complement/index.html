why is 2’s complement?

2’s complement is a method of how to represent numbers in computer memory.with 2’s complement,we could store values,do arithmetic compution and so on.

there are many ways to represent numbers,howerever ,both of them are not that perfect,2’s complement overcomes these defaults to a great extent,that’s why it has been widely used now.
